# HackIndia-Spark-7-2025-She-Codes
Call It Done - AI Meeting Companion
Your intelligent meeting assistant that transcribes, summarizes, and organizes meetings so you can focus on the conversation.
OVERVIEW
Call It Done is a comprehensive AI meeting companion that transforms the way professionals handle meetings. Our platform eliminates the need for manual note-taking and administrative tasks by providing real-time transcription, smart summaries, and seamless integration with productivity tools.
Meetings are essential for collaboration but often come with significant overhead:
-Time spent taking notes distracts from active participation
-Important details are frequently missed or forgotten
-Meeting insights remain siloed and difficult to share
-Follow-up tasks and action items get lost
-Organizing and accessing meeting information is cumbersome
OUR SOLUTION
Call It Done combines advanced speech recognition, natural language processing, and AI summarization to create a comprehensive meeting assistant:
Web Application Features
Meeting Transcription: Upload recordings for accurate, speaker-separated transcriptions
AI-Powered Summaries: Receive concise meeting summaries highlighting key points
Action Item Extraction: Automatically identify and track tasks and commitments
Notion Integration: Sync meeting notes directly to your workspace
Meeting Management: Store and organize meeting details including:
-Attendee information
-Meeting links
-Date and time
-Agendas and outcomes
CHROME EXTENSION FEATURE
Real-Time Transcription: Live transcription during any online meeting
Meeting Capture: Works across popular platforms (Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, etc.)
Instant Summaries: Get meeting highlights immediately after calls end
TECHNOLOGY STACK
Frontend: CSS, HTML 
Backend: JavaScript , Python
